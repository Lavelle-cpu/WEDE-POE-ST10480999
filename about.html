Our Story

CS Sports was established in 2019 by Ciaran Smith, a certified personal trainer and sports rehabilitation specialist. The business started as a one-on-one personal training service in local parks and gyms across Cape Town. Over time, the brand has grown into a respected training and wellness company offering in-person and online fitness coaching, group training, and nutrition plans. CS Sports is committed to helping clients achieve their physical goals in a safe, supportive, and motivating environment.

Our Mission

To empower individuals through expert-guided training programs that improve fitness, build confidence, and promote lifelong wellness.

Our Vision

To be South Africa's leading personal training brand known for innovation, transformation, and sustainable fitness solutions.
